About Me

Find out a little bit about me to see if we would be a good fit for working together.

My name is Kevin Dakin and I am a freelance graphic designer currently living in central California.

I have had an immense passion in design for the last eight years, when I first discovered the wonderful world of graphic design. Since then, I have been largely self-taught through exploration and a drive for constantly learning new things and advancing my abilities. I am currently attending California State University, Fresno pursuing a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in Graphic Design with an emphasis in print design. When I am not designing, you can usually find me reading design articles or enjoying a delicious craft beer. I commit myself to staying up on current design trends to provide the most modern designs to my clients. I am captivated with design and I engrossed with the fine details that turn a good design into a great design.

I specialize in brand & identity design, print design, and web design.

With design, I strive to solve conceptual design problems with effective visual communication. My goal in all of my work is to completely fulfill the client's brief with a compelling and useful design. Whether it is a branding package or a website, I aim to solve your design problems and communicate the best message to your customers. Good and effective design is a crucial piece to a successful business. A great logo will make your company recognizable and make it stand out from the competition. A well-designed advertisement will grab the attention of potential customers. An intuitive website with great usability will help your customers learn information about your business, enabling your company to grow. I would love to work with you on any of your future design projects. Feel free to contact me today.
